BBQ Pasta

This dish is a comfort food of mine. Super simple but surprisngly tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of your favorite pasta (Campanelle for me)
  • Your favorite BBQ sauce (Sweet Baby Ray's Honey reccomended)
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Add pasta and cook until al dente (cook time will vary by pasta type)
  3. Drain pasta and serve into a bowl
  4. Add BBQ sauce
  5. Grate on Parmesean cheese
  6. Top with red pepper flakes(if using)
  7. Stir to combine
  8. Enjoy!
