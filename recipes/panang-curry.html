Panang Curry

A super easy and tasty meal. Great to impress friends/family or to enjoy on your own for a few days.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1" piece of ginger minced
  • 1 4oz can Panang curry paste
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • Coconut oil
  • Greek yogurt

Steps

  1. Add rice and 2 cups water into rice cooker and start the rice cooker
  2. Cut bell peppers into 1 inch chunks
  3. Finely dice the onion
  4. Cut chicken into 1" chunks
  5. Add some coconut oil to a dutch oven on medium/high heat
  6. Once the oil is hot add the chicken and cook, turning the pieces only after they have browned on the other side. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  7. Remove the chicken with a slotted spoon or tongs and transfer to a bowl or plate lined with a paper towel
  8. Add a little more coconut oil if needed and toss in the onions
  9. After 2 minutes add in the garlic, ginger, and bell peppers
  10. Cook for about 5 minutes then push everthing to the side of the dutch oven
  11. Add the can of curry paste to the pot and cook for another minute or two until the paste becomes fragrant and releases some oil
  12. Add the chicken back in and mix everything to combine
  13. Add in the cocounut milk and peanut butter and a 1/2 cup of water. Stir to combine
  14. Bring to a simmer and cook covered for 15 minutes
  15. Remove the top and simmer until desired consistency is obtained
  16. Plate up. Add rice into a bowl and spoon in curry. Top with greek yogurt and enjoy!
